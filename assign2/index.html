Bruno Pizzeria

Bruno Pizzeria is the oldest pizzeria in garbagetown, this is because it is the only pizzeria in town.

We will make any pizza but have a range of set menu items for your convenience.
To order a custom pizza please select at least 3 of the following ingredients:
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Margherita

  • Margherita but you regret it tomorrow

  • Hawaiian without pinapple

  • Ham and cheese
